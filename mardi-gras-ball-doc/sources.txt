KEY FINDINGS:
-------------

1. TIMING CHALLENGE: Most formal Mardi Gras balls occur on Mardi Gras evening
   (February 17, 2026) rather than the weekend before (Feb 13-15).

2. PRIVACY: Many prestigious balls (Rex, Comus, Proteus) are private events
   requiring krewe membership or invitations. Public ticket information is
   intentionally not published.

3. EARLY PLANNING STAGE: As of January 11, 2026, many krewes have not yet
   published detailed 2026 ball information on their public websites.

4. PARADE vs BALL: The weekend before Mardi Gras focuses heavily on parades.
   The Krewe of Endymion Extravaganza (Feb 14) is a notable exception - it
   combines parade and celebration in one major event.

5. CONTACT REQUIRED: For most current and complete information, direct contact
   with individual krewe offices is necessary.
